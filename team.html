WatchDog - In-Command (IC)

Name:
Gary Lalonde
Active Since:
Spring 2008
Member Status:
Founder

Background:

Served almost 40 yrs with National Defence, 22 yrs in uniform as a Military Policeman and another 17 yrs as a Civilian Investigator with the CAF Prevost Marshal's Office in Ottawa.

Gear Used:

  • Ares Ameba Honey Badger CQB (M4).
  • G&G CM16 (M4) Carbine.
  • KJ Works 1911 MEU.

Why I chose MILSIM:

Military Simulation is by far more exciting, challenging and realistic than any other type of game play.   MilSim promotes teamwork and esprit de corps between teammates and makes for more rewarding result at the end of the day's play.

Our team has a wealth of Military experience and knowledge and the training we provide our teammates involving individual combat skills and unit combat skills during the year, helps provide a more structured, cohesive successful team and provides a more significant challenge to our adversaries.
